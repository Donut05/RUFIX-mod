Here you will fnd all the terrain assets to place in your terrain.

Move them and change the colors.


Move

This will allow you to move placed assets on the terrain.

The asset list will open below when you use Move, Rotate or Scale.

Rotate

This will allow you to rotate any placed asset.

The asset list will open below when you use Move, Rotate or Scale.

Scale

This will allow you to scale the size of any placed asset.

The asset list will open below when you use Move, Rotate or Scale.

Asset Library

In this list you will fnd all the terrain props like rocks and trees

to decorate your terrain with. Simply select any asset you want in the list

and drag it out on the terrain.

The top bar is for categories; Trees, Rocks, Foliage, Manmade, Mod assets.

Properties

This window allows you to set the color of a placed asset

as well as setting its exact position, rotation and scale.

Color

In the color window you select what color you want to paint your selected

asset.
